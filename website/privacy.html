Your privacy matters.
Outly is built to connect real people safely in real life. We protect your personal information and only use data necessary to improve the platform, events, communities and safety systems.

1. Information We Collect

When using Outly, we may collect:

  • Profile information such as username, profile image and bio
  • Email address and authentication data
  • Activities, events and participation data
  • Messages and community chat content
  • Location data for nearby discovery features
  • Device and technical information for security and stability

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide activities, events and social features
  • To improve safety and moderation systems
  • To prevent abuse, spam and fake accounts
  • To personalize nearby recommendations
  • To maintain platform stability and technical support

3. Safety & Moderation

Outly may review reports, chats, profiles and activities when necessary to protect users and enforce community rules.

Dangerous, illegal or abusive behavior may lead to moderation actions including warnings, suspensions or permanent bans.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal user data.

Certain services such as authentication, hosting, cloud storage, notifications and analytics may be processed by trusted third-party providers.

5. Account & Data Deletion

Users may request deletion of their account and personal data through support at any time.

Some moderation or legal records may be retained temporarily for safety, fraud prevention and legal compliance purposes.
